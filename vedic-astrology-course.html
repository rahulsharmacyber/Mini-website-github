Course Overview

What Is Vedic Astrology (Jyotish)?

Vedic astrology, known in Sanskrit as Jyotish or the “science of light,” is one of the world's oldest astrological traditions. It uses the sidereal zodiac, Navagraha, twelve Bhavas and twenty-seven Nakshatras to map the blueprint of a person's life.

Unlike Western astrology, Vedic astrology places deep emphasis on the Moon sign, Lagna and predictive tools such as the Vimshottari Dasha system. Its connection with karma, dharma and cosmic timing makes it both spiritual and practical.

This professional Vedic astrology course teaches birth chart analysis, divisional charts, planetary periods and Vedic remedies — skills used by practising astrologers across India and worldwide.
